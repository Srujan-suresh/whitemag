Human Resources Executive

Company: White Mag Studio

Location: Coimbatore – Hopes College

Employment Type: Full-time

About Us

At White Mag Studio, we are passionate about creating world-class VFX, CGI, and creative content. Our team thrives on innovation, collaboration, and pushing artistic boundaries. As we continue to grow, we are looking for an HR Executive who can help us build a strong people-focused culture and support our expanding creative workforce.

Role Overview

The HR Executive will be responsible for handling end-to-end HR functions – from recruitment to employee engagement – ensuring that our studio remains a vibrant and productive space for artists and professionals.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Drive the recruitment process: sourcing, interviewing, and onboarding talent in VFX/CGI/creative domains.
  • Manage employee records, payroll, and attendance.
  • Build and execute employee engagement initiatives to enhance workplace culture.
  • Support performance management, appraisals, and training programs.
  • Ensure compliance with HR policies, labor laws, and studio guidelines.
  • Address employee concerns and foster a positive, collaborative environment.

Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in HR, Business Administration, or related field.
  • 1–3 years of experience in HR (experience in media/VFX/creative industry is a plus).
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Ability to handle confidential information and manage multiple priorities.
  • Knowledge of HR tools and software.

What We Offer

  • A chance to work with a passionate and creative team.
  • Competitive salary and growth opportunities.
  • A collaborative, artist-friendly work culture.
  • Exposure to exciting projects in the global VFX and creative industry.
