WHO WE ARE

WhiteMag Studio is a global VFX and animation studio redefining the art of visual storytelling. We fuse cutting-edge technology with world-class artistry to create imagery that inspires, immerses, and endures — from blockbuster Hollywood films and high-end episodic content to music, advertising, and immersive experiences.

Our team of exceptional artists and supervisors collaborate closely with filmmakers, studios, and brands to craft visuals that elevate narrative and emotion. Whether integrating complex CG environments, perfecting digital doubles, or achieving seamless compositing, every frame reflects our pursuit of cinematic excellence.

Respected for precision, creativity, and storytelling power, WhiteMag continues to push the boundaries of what’s possible in visual effects — shaping moments that are not just seen, but felt.

At our core, we are creators driven by imagination, innovation, and a passion to deliver visuals that are truly A Cut Above Reality.

OUR CORE SERVICES

At WhiteMag Studio, we craft visual experiences that blend creativity and technology — bringing imagination to life through cinematic visuals and cutting-edge artistry.
Rotoscoping & Paint

High-precision matte generation, paint fixes, beauty clean-ups, and marker removals executed with seamless detail and production-grade accuracy.

Compositing Services

Layering reality and imagination with finesse. Our compositing artists integrate CG, live-action, and FX to craft believable, cinematic frames.

Matchmoving & Camera Tracking

Frame-accurate 3D camera tracking and object matching to ensure CG elements align perfectly with the physical world.

Green Screen / Chroma Keying

Precision keying and spill suppression for flawless background replacements, virtual production integration, and green screen shoots.

Additional VFX Enhancements

Advanced visual effects techniques including cleanup, tracking, and seamless integration to elevate every project to cinematic quality.
